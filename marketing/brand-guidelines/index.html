    AeroVine Brand Guidelines¶

    Brand Overview¶

    AeroVine's brand identity reflects our position at the intersection of cutting-edge technology and the timeless tradition of winemaking.

    Brand Colors¶

    Primary Colors¶

    • Purple (#5D3A7A) - Grapes/Wine - Primary Brand Color
    • Blue (#4A90E2) - Technology/Reliability - Secondary Brand Color
    • Gold (#D4A574) - Harvest/Success - Accent Color
    • Gray (#7A8B99) - Terroir/Grounding - Neutral Color

    Color Usage¶

    • Purple: Primary headings, logo elements, brand accents, grape cluster imagery
    • Blue: Company name text, technology features, call-to-action buttons, links
    • Gold: Success indicators, positive metrics, accent highlights, harvest imagery
    • Gray: Body text, secondary information, borders, neutral backgrounds

    Typography¶

    • Headings: System Sans-Serif (SF Pro, Segoe UI, Roboto), Bold (700)
    • Body Text: System Sans-Serif, 16px (1em), Line Height: 1.6
    • Company Name: Bold, Blue (#4A90E2), Letter Spacing: -1px

    Logo¶

    The AeroVine logo combines: - A drone icon at the top - A grape cluster in the middle - "AeroVine" text at the bottom in blue

    Brand Values¶

    • Innovation: Embracing cutting-edge AI and drone technology
    • Safety First: Prioritizing safety in all operations
    • Customer Focus: Deep commitment to wine industry expertise
    • Growth Mindset: Continuous learning and improvement

    Visual Identity¶

    For complete brand guidelines including logo variations, spacing, and usage examples, see the Brand Board.
