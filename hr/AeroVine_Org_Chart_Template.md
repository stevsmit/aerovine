## Key Considerations for Scaling

### Communication Structure
- **Daily Standups:** Department-level (Engineering, Operations)
- **Weekly All-Hands:** Company-wide updates
- **Bi-weekly 1:1s:** Manager-employee check-ins
- **Monthly Leadership:** Executive team strategic planning

### Decision-Making Framework
- **Strategic:** CEO + Executive Team
- **Tactical:** Department Heads
- **Operational:** Individual contributors with manager approval

### Culture & Values
- **Innovation:** Encourage experimentation and learning
- **Safety First:** UAS operations and data security
- **Customer Focus:** Wine industry expertise and service
- **Growth Mindset:** Support professional development

### Compensation Philosophy
- **Competitive Base:** Market-rate salaries
- **Equity Participation:** Stock options for all employees
- **Performance Bonuses:** Tied to company and individual goals
- **Benefits:** Health, dental, vision, 401(k) match

3. **Remote Work:** Some roles can be remote (engineering, product, finance)
4. **Field Work:** Operations roles require Napa Valley presence
5. **Certifications:** Drone pilots must maintain FAA Part 107 certification
