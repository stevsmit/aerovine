    Key Roles for a Drone Technology Company¶

    Engineering Roles¶

    Senior Software Engineer - AI/ML Platform¶

    Department: Engineering
    Reports to: Engineering Manager
    Location: Napa Valley, CA (Hybrid/Remote options)
    Employment Type: Full-time, Exempt

    Job Summary¶

    AeroVine is seeking a Senior Software Engineer to lead development of our AI-powered drone platform. You'll architect and build systems that process agricultural data, train machine learning models, and deliver actionable insights to wine industry clients.

    Key Responsibilities¶

    • Design and develop scalable AI/ML systems for agricultural data analysis
    • Build and maintain data pipelines for processing multispectral imagery
    • Train and deploy machine learning models for crop health analysis
    • Collaborate with product and operations teams to define technical requirements
    • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to code reviews
    • Ensure system reliability, performance, and security
    • Stay current with AI/ML and drone technology trends

    Required Qualifications¶

    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent experience)
    • 5+ years of software engineering experience
    • Strong proficiency in Python, with experience in ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
    • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
    • Knowledge of computer vision and image processing
    • Experience with data pipelines and ETL processes
    • Strong problem-solving and communication skills

    Preferred Qualifications¶

    • Master's degree or PhD in Computer Science, AI, or related field
    • Experience with agricultural technology or precision agriculture
    • Knowledge of drone/UAS systems and regulations
    • Experience with geospatial data and GIS systems
    • Previous startup experience

    Compensation¶

    • Salary: $140,000 - $180,000
    • Equity: 0.15% - 0.25%
    • Benefits: Health, dental, vision, 401(k) match, unlimited PTO

    Mid-Level Software Engineer - Platform Development¶

    Department: Engineering
    Reports to: Engineering Manager
    Location: Napa Valley, CA (Hybrid/Remote options)
    Employment Type: Full-time, Exempt

    Job Summary¶

    Join AeroVine's engineering team to build features and integrations for our drone platform. You'll work on APIs, data visualization, and customer-facing applications that help vintners make data-driven decisions.

    Key Responsibilities¶

    • Develop and maintain backend APIs and services
    • Build frontend features for data visualization dashboards
    • Integrate with third-party systems and APIs
    • Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
    • Participate in agile development processes
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams
    • Debug and resolve production issues

    Required Qualifications¶

    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent experience)
    • 3+ years of software engineering experience
    • Proficiency in Python, JavaScript, or similar languages
    • Experience with web frameworks (Django, Flask, React, or similar)
    • Knowledge of databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB)
    • Understanding of RESTful API design
    • Strong collaboration and communication skills

    Preferred Qualifications¶

    • Experience with geospatial data visualization
    • Knowledge of agricultural or drone technology
    • Experience with cloud infrastructure
    • Previous startup experience

    Compensation¶

    • Salary: $110,000 - $140,000
    • Equity: 0.10% - 0.15%
    • Benefits: Health, dental, vision, 401(k) match, unlimited PTO

    DevOps Engineer¶

    Department: Engineering
    Reports to: Engineering Manager
    Location: Napa Valley, CA (Remote-friendly)
    Employment Type: Full-time, Exempt

    Job Summary¶

    AeroVine needs a DevOps Engineer to build and maintain our cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment systems. You'll ensure our platform is reliable, scalable, and secure as we grow.

    Key Responsibilities¶

    • Design and maintain cloud infrastructure (AWS preferred)
    • Build and optimize CI/CD pipelines
    • Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
    • Ensure system security and compliance
    • Automate deployment and scaling processes
    • Troubleshoot production infrastructure issues
    • Document infrastructure and processes

    Required Qualifications¶

    • 3+ years of DevOps or infrastructure engineering experience
    • Strong experience with AWS (EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda, etc.)
    • Proficiency with infrastructure as code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
    • Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
    • Knowledge of CI/CD tools (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, CircleCI)
    • Understanding of networking and security best practices
    • Strong scripting skills (Bash, Python)

    Preferred Qualifications¶

    • AWS certifications
    • Experience with monitoring tools (Datadog, New Relic, Prometheus)
    • Knowledge of database administration
    • Previous startup experience

    Compensation¶

    • Salary: $120,000 - $150,000
    • Equity: 0.10% - 0.15%
    • Benefits: Health, dental, vision, 401(k) match, unlimited PTO

    Operations Roles¶

    Field Operations Manager¶

    Department: Operations
    Reports to: Head of Operations (COO)
    Location: Napa Valley, CA (On-site required)
    Employment Type: Full-time, Exempt

    Job Summary¶

    Lead AeroVine's field operations team, managing drone flight operations, data collection, and customer service delivery. You'll ensure safe, efficient, and high-quality service to our vineyard clients.

    Key Responsibilities¶

    • Manage field operations team and drone pilots
    • Schedule and coordinate flight operations across multiple vineyards
    • Ensure compliance with FAA regulations and safety protocols
    • Maintain drone fleet and equipment
    • Train and certify new pilots
    • Coordinate with customers on service delivery
    • Analyze operational efficiency and implement improvements
    • Manage field operations budget

    Required Qualifications¶

    • 5+ years of operations management experience
    • FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate (or ability to obtain)
    • Experience with drone/UAS operations
    • Strong leadership and team management skills
    • Excellent organizational and communication skills
    • Valid driver's license and ability to travel to vineyard sites
    • Knowledge of agricultural operations (preferred)

    Preferred Qualifications¶

    • Experience in precision agriculture or agricultural technology
    • Knowledge of wine industry and vineyard operations
    • Previous startup experience
    • Experience with fleet management

    Compensation¶

    • Salary: $85,000 - $110,000
    • Equity: 0.10% - 0.15%
    • Benefits: Health, dental, vision, 401(k) match, unlimited PTO
    • Vehicle allowance for field operations

    Drone Pilot / Field Operations Specialist¶

    Department: Operations
    Reports to: Field Operations Manager
    Location: Napa Valley, CA (Field-based)
    Employment Type: Full-time, Non-Exempt

    Job Summary¶

    Operate AeroVine's drones to collect agricultural data for vineyard clients. You'll be the face of AeroVine in the field, ensuring safe flights and high-quality data collection.

    Key Responsibilities¶

    • Conduct drone flights for agricultural data collection
    • Follow FAA regulations and AeroVine safety protocols
    • Maintain and inspect drone equipment
    • Process and upload flight data
    • Communicate with vineyard managers and clients
    • Document flight operations and data collection
    • Assist with equipment maintenance and repairs

    Required Qualifications¶

    • FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate (required)
    • 1+ years of commercial drone piloting experience
    • Strong attention to detail and safety awareness
    • Excellent communication and customer service skills
    • Ability to work outdoors in various weather conditions
    • Valid driver's license and reliable transportation
    • Physical ability to carry and operate drone equipment

    Preferred Qualifications¶

    • Experience with agricultural or precision agriculture applications
    • Knowledge of multispectral imaging and sensors
    • Experience with DJI or similar commercial drone platforms
    • Knowledge of wine industry or vineyard operations

    Compensation¶

    • Salary: $50,000 - $65,000
    • Overtime eligible
    • Equity: 0.05% - 0.10%
    • Benefits: Health, dental, vision, 401(k) match, unlimited PTO
    • Vehicle allowance and equipment provided

    Sales & Customer Success Roles¶

    Sales Manager¶

    Department: Sales
    Reports to: Head of Operations (COO)
    Location: Napa Valley, CA (Field-based with office)
    Employment Type: Full-time, Exempt

    Job Summary¶

    Build and lead AeroVine's sales team, developing relationships with vineyard owners and wine industry professionals to drive revenue growth across Napa, Sonoma, and Mendocino counties.

    Key Responsibilities¶

    • Develop and execute sales strategy
    • Build and manage sales pipeline
    • Lead sales team and set targets
    • Conduct sales calls and presentations
    • Negotiate contracts and pricing
    • Attend industry events and networking
    • Collaborate with marketing on lead generation
    • Track sales metrics and forecast revenue

    Required Qualifications¶

    • 5+ years of B2B sales experience
    • 2+ years of sales management experience
    • Proven track record of meeting/exceeding sales targets
    • Strong relationship-building and negotiation skills
    • Excellent communication and presentation skills
    • Ability to travel to client sites
    • Valid driver's license

    Preferred Qualifications¶

    • Experience selling to agricultural or wine industry
    • Knowledge of precision agriculture or drone technology
    • Existing network in Napa/Sonoma wine industry
    • Previous startup experience
    • Experience with CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot)

    Compensation¶

    • Base Salary: $80,000 - $100,000
    • Commission: Uncapped, target OTE $150,000 - $200,000
    • Equity: 0.15% - 0.25%
    • Benefits: Health, dental, vision, 401(k) match, unlimited PTO

    HR/People Operations Roles¶

    HR Director¶

    Department: HR/People Operations
    Reports to: CFO
    Location: Napa Valley, CA (Hybrid)
    Employment Type: Full-time, Exempt

    Job Summary¶

    Lead AeroVine's people operations, building HR infrastructure, culture, and processes to support rapid growth from 7 to 25+ employees.

    Key Responsibilities¶

    • Develop and implement HR policies and procedures
    • Manage recruitment and hiring processes
    • Oversee employee onboarding and offboarding
    • Administer benefits and compensation programs
    • Handle employee relations and conflict resolution
    • Ensure compliance with employment laws and regulations
    • Develop performance management processes
    • Build company culture and employee engagement programs
    • Manage HR systems and records

    Required Qualifications¶

    • 5+ years of HR experience, with 2+ years in leadership
    • Bachelor's degree in HR, Business, or related field
    • Strong knowledge of employment law and compliance
    • Experience with HRIS systems
    • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
    • Ability to handle sensitive and confidential information

    Preferred Qualifications¶

    • HR certification (SHRM-CP, PHR, or similar)
    • Previous startup or high-growth company experience
    • Experience scaling teams from 10-50 employees
    • Knowledge of California employment law

    Compensation¶

    • Salary: $100,000 - $130,000
    • Equity: 0.15% - 0.25%
    • Benefits: Health, dental, vision, 401(k) match, unlimited PTO

    General Application Information¶

    Application Process¶

    1. Submit resume and cover letter
    2. Initial phone/video screening
    3. Technical assessment (for engineering roles)
    4. Interview with hiring manager
    5. Interview with team members
    6. Final interview with executive team
    7. Reference checks
    8. Offer extended

    AeroVine Benefits¶

    • Health Insurance: Medical, dental, and vision coverage
    • Retirement: 401(k) with company match
    • Equity: Stock options for all employees
    • PTO: Unlimited paid time off
    • Professional Development: Learning budget and conference attendance
    • Equipment: Laptop and necessary tools provided
    • Wellness: Gym membership or wellness stipend
    • Location: Beautiful Napa Valley office with vineyard views
