Color Usage Guidelines

Purple (#5D3A7A)

  • Primary headings
  • Logo elements
  • Brand accents
  • Grape cluster imagery

Blue (#4A90E2)

  • Company name text
  • Technology features
  • Call-to-action buttons
  • Links and highlights

Gold (#D4A574)

  • Success indicators
  • Positive metrics
  • Accent highlights
  • Harvest imagery

Gray (#7A8B99)

  • Body text
  • Secondary information
  • Borders and dividers
  • Neutral backgrounds
